About Us

Greta and Amelia are two sisters from South Jordan, Utah, who have turned their shared passion for photography into a thriving business. Specializing in portraits, weddings, and events, they've been capturing beautiful moments for almost three years. They work together seamlessly, from snapping the perfect shots to editing each photo to ensure it looks its absolute best. Whether it’s a milestone celebration or a quiet portrait, Greta and Amelia approach each shot with a unique blend of creativity and care, bringing their clients' visions to life with authenticity and heart.

Hi, I'm Greta, a mom of four and the creative force behind Amelia and Greta Photography. What began as a way to capture my own family's special moments has evolved into a passion for helping others preserve their memories. I specialize in family portraits and lifestyle photography, focusing on the genuine, heartfelt moments that make your story unique. As a mother, I understand how quickly time passes, and I’m here to help you freeze those fleeting moments into memories that will last a lifetime.
